Services

We can provide legal support services for all types of residential conveyancing cases and process the tasks from start to end or work only in the areas you require. We have experienced professionals to deal with Legal and Completion tasks.

Case Creation and Pre-Contract Stage

Case Management System Setup:
We proficiently set up cases in leading case mangagement software, ensuring seamless workflow management.

Property Searches:
We order a variety of essential property searches, including Bankruptcy search(K17), Official search with priority of whole or of part of the title(OS1/OS2), HM Land Registry (HMLR) and others.

Exchange of Contracts

Managing Contract Exchange:
We oversee the critical phase of contract exchange, ensuring all legal and procedural aspects are meticulously handled.

Mortgage Related Documents:
We draft necessary mortgage-related documents such as Mortgage Advice Letters and Help to Buy (HTB) Forms 1 & 2, facilitating mortgage transactions effectively.

Completion

Completion Pack Preparation:
Our team prepares comprehensive completion packs, ensuring all necessary documentation and procedures are in place for a successful transaction conclusion.

Re-Mortgage and Transfer of Equity:
We manage end-to-end re-mortgage and transfer of equity transactions, ensuring efficiency and compliance with all regulatory requirements.

Post-Completion

AP1 and SDLT Forms
We expertly handle all post-completion tasks, including drafting and submitting AP1 forms for HM Land Registry to register ownership or mortgage changes. Our team ensures compliance with current regulations and manages the submission of Stamp Duty Land Tax (SDLT) forms, calculating and reporting tax accurately. Our aim is to ensure a smooth transition from completion to registration, minimizing issues and meeting all legal requirements efficiently.
